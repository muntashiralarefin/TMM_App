Overview on
‘Tourists’ Mind Mapping and Experience’
Survey at Cox’s Bazar


UNDP Accelerator Lab has conducted tourist mind mapping surveys in different tourism-centric districts of the country on tourists to understand their satisfaction and expectations. This initiative is ongoing in one-after-another selected districts.

Objective

1. REALIZATION: To realize the satisfaction scenario and reasons of dissatisfaction of tourists
2. SUGGESTION: To know the expectation and recommendations of tourist
3. COOPERATION: To make proper plans for the improvement of our tourism sector and to cooperate the relevant authority
4. DEVELOPMENT: To inform the stakeholders about the issues and planning for the proper execution

Community Based Tourism (CBT)

Most of the tourists of Cox’s Bazar don’t know about community based tourism. So, it need to make more popular.
Also, authority need to promote local culture and heritage for tourists attraction.

Vacation Package & Tour Operator

In vacation package, most of the tourists want hotel and food facility as these are the most complicated part of a tour. Some tourists want other facilities as well.
Tourists who visit Cox’s Bazar 88% don’t receive the service of tour operator usually. They try to visit on their own.

Hotel Cost & Expectation

Most of the tourists (53%) think that the hotel cost is expected in Cox’s Bazar. Similarly, around 43% tourists thing the cost is high.
Besides, majority of the tourists expect safe environment and good room service.
Resent condition and review of service receiver should be available in online.

Midnight Travel, Safety & Security

Almost two-thirds tourists prefer travel in midnight and they think it is safe for them, which is a positive sign.
Most of the tourists (79%) are satisfied on overall security.

92% tourists of Cox’s Bazar know about tourist police.
But, since the rate of harassment is very low, the tourists don’t receive direct service of tourist police frequently. Only 3% tourist need direct service from tourist police.

Transport System

We can see, the main reason for tourists’ dissatisfaction on transport is high rent (62%). Insecurity also a major issue.
But, overall tourists are satisfied on both internal (tomtom/riksha) and highway transport service. Dissatisfaction on internal transport is little bit high.

Street Food

In our survey, more than half (55%) tourists don’t like street food in Cox’s Bazar.
Most of the tourists (86%) expects healthy food from street food shops. The main reason of their dissatisfaction is high price.

Spot Cleanliness

30% tourists think, tourists spots of Cox’s Bazar are not clean enough, which is a concern for us.
Though majority of the tourists (86%) think that, they are cooperating to keep the spots clean.
